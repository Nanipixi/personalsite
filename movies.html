Nani's Top Three Nostalgia Movies

My Favorite Comfort Films

Coraline

It's a nostalgic film for me that reminds me to look for magic sometimes.

Mononoke Hime

This anime is my favorite out of the studio ghibli films I watched as a child. The art and approach to the topic have always been beautifully done in my eyes.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

My family and I greatly enjoy this movie for both christmas and halloween. It's a very fun movie to watch for everyone!
